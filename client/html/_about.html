About Hiking Tool

The goal of Hiking Tool is to assist you in better preparing for your hikes, while also allowing you to improve your hiking skills by learning from each experience.

Prepare

Add a trip, and before leaving, fill out a "pre-trip" form, providing information like the weather, potential hazards, map location and more. See a round-trip time estimate, based upon your distance and elevation gain.

Learn

Upon returning from a trip, fill out a "post-trip" form, providing information such as actual travel times, actual weather experienced, wildlife observed, hazards experienced and general notes for next time.

Improve

By asking certain questions, the pre-trip form helps you prepare for your hike. By filling out the post-trip forms, you can look back on your hiking history and learn how to avoid past issues or better prepare.

Safer and Smarter

Knowing the weather, haivng the right gear, and knowing your travel times can make a big difference when trying to enjoy the outdoors. At times, preparing for a hike can feel hectic, and sometimes we might forget items accidentally, or forget to garner important information before departing into areas away from cell phone service.

Hiking Tool aims to help your outdoor trip prepartion by having you fill out a form before and after your experience. Each asks questions that will aid in your preparation and awareness, and as reports may be viewed any time, you can learn and prepare better for next time. The aim of Hiking tool is to help you hike safer and smarter.

Technical Stuff:

Currently, Hiking Tool is:

  • Only browser-based (no mobile-specific application), although the website is mobile friendly.
  • Hiking tool may evolve into a sister mobile application.
  • Hiking tool's features may evolve to become more robust or to provide greater use.
  • This is an MVC, MEAN stack application, utilizing the following technologies:
    • MongoDB database
    • ExpressJS framework
    • Angular JS framework
    • NodeJS server
    • Angular UI Bootstrap with Darkly Bootstrap theme
