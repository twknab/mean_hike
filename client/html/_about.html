About Hiking Tool

The goal of Hiking Tool is to assist you in better preparing for your hikes, while also allowing you to improve your hiking skills by learning from each experience.

Prepare

Add a trip -- before leaving, fill out a "pre-trip" and provide information like the weather, potential hazards, a map location and more. See a round-trip time estimate based upon your distance and elevation gain.

Learn

Upon returning from a trip, fill out a "post-trip" and provide information such as actual travel times, actual weather experienced, wildlife observed, hazards experienced and general notes for next time.

Improve

By asking certain questions, the pre-trip helps you prepare for your hike. By filling out a post-trip, you can look back on your hiking history and learn how to avoid past issues or better prepare.
